## 1. CURRENT PRICING CATALOG (2026)
Agents (human or AI) must only reference the following active plans when proposing migrations. Do not invent plan names or pricing.

* **Basic 5G Lite:** $30/mo. Includes 5GB high-speed data, unlimited domestic calls/texts. No international roaming. Best for low-usage customers.
* **Standard Unlimited:** $55/mo. Includes unlimited 5G data, calls, and texts. Best for average-to-high domestic users experiencing bill shock on legacy pay-per-minute plans.
* **Global Unlimited Pro:** $85/mo. Includes unlimited domestic data, plus unlimited calling to 85+ countries and free data roaming in 50+ countries. 

## 2. CHURN DRIVER INTERVENTION MATRIX
When an account is flagged for high churn risk, use the following approved interventions based on the primary friction driver.

### 2.1. Driver: High Customer Service Calls (Technical Friction)
* **Diagnosis:** The customer is experiencing repeated network drops or hardware failure.
* **Approved Strategy:** 1. Immediately escalate the account to a "Tier 2 Dedicated Technical Manager."
  2. Offer a one-time "Service Apology Credit" of $20 on their next bill.
  3. Offer a 15% discount on their current plan for the next 3 months to rebuild trust.

### 2.2. Driver: High International Charges without an International Plan
* **Diagnosis:** The customer is experiencing severe "bill shock" from pay-as-you-go international rates.
* **Approved Strategy:**
  1. Explain that their current usage profile does not match their plan.
  2. Offer to explicitly waive 50% of last month's international overage charges.
  3. **Condition:** The waiver is ONLY valid if they agree to immediately upgrade to the "Global Unlimited Pro" ($85/mo) plan.

### 2.3. Driver: High Day Cost Per Minute (Legacy Plan Inefficiency)
* **Diagnosis:** The customer is on an outdated, uncapped usage plan and paying too much for basic daily minutes.
* **Approved Strategy:**
  1. Conduct a "Free Account Audit."
  2. Propose a migration to the "Standard Unlimited" ($55/mo) plan.
  3. Offer a "Price Lock Guarantee" ensuring their rate will not increase for 12 months.

### 2.4. Driver: Low General Usage
* **Diagnosis:** The customer is paying for a premium plan but barely using minutes or data. They are likely shopping for cheaper competitors.
* **Approved Strategy:**
  1. Highlight unused value and offer a 3-month free subscription to our partner streaming service, "StreamFlix Plus."
  2. If they still wish to cancel, proactively offer a downgrade to the "Basic 5G Lite" ($30/mo) plan to retain the account rather than losing them entirely.

## 3. ETHICAL AI, LEGAL & COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES
All retention offers, whether drafted by human agents or Automated AI workflows, MUST adhere to the following compliance regulations. Violations are subject to regulatory fines.

* **Financial Hardship:** If a customer explicitly states they are leaving due to job loss, bankruptcy, or severe financial hardship, DO NOT pressure them with upsells. Offer a temporary 3-month account suspension without penalty.
* **Cooling-Off Period:** All accepted retention offers and contract extensions are legally subject to a 14-day "Cooling-Off Period" where the customer can cancel without penalty.
* **Mandatory AI Disclosure:** Any email, chat, or report generated by the LangGraph AI System must append the following disclaimer at the bottom of the correspondence: 
> *"Disclaimer: This retention plan was generated by an AI assistant analyzing your usage metrics to find the best available savings. All offers are subject to final account verification and standard Terms of Service."*
